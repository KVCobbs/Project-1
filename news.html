Police officer shoots unarmed Black Man

Andre Hill was a 47 year old man who was shot unarmed, He was holding a phone in his left hand. Police were responding to a non-emergency 911 call (a noise complaint) when they saw Hill leaving a garage, he was shot 3 times in the leg and once in his chest. His death was ruled a homicide caused by multiple gunshot wounds.
